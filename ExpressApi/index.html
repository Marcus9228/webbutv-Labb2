In browser:

"/pokemons" to see all pokemons

"/pokemon/id" to see specific pokemon

"/items" to see all items

"/item/id" to see specific item

In postman:

"/pokemon" to post new pokemon

"/pokemon/id" to update pokemon

"/pokemon/id" to delete pokemon

"/item" to post new item

"/item/id" to update item

"/item/id" to delete item
